Supporting Homeless Children

There are millions of children around us, who have no safe home, no functional families, no healthy meals a day.. They have no hope for the future, beyond just surviving. Can we lend a helping hand, a ray of hope, a sparkle of joy? ATMA Foundation reaches out to children who are in need of care and protection, and provides them a safe place to stay, healthy food and regular schooling. We aggressively pursue an individual care plan for each child, identifying her interest and talents, helping her figure out what she wants to do with her life. We empower her with the skill set necessary to pursue her professional aspirations. We help her transform into a leader in her chosen career field.

How we make it possible?

ATMA Uyare is an online learning support programme for underprivileged children, with special focus on children in Children's Homes across Kerala.

ATMA Uyare (educational wing of ATMA Foundation) provides learning support to underprivileged children, with special focus on those living in Child Care Institutions. Provides free online classes on different subjects through subject experts for the homeless and underprivileged children.
